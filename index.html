About us

The emergency room of a hospital is a bustling and critical area where patients with urgent medical needs receive immediate care. It is staffed by a team of dedicated healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, and technicians, who work tirelessly to stabilize patients and provide essential treatments. With its state-of-the-art equipment and round-the-clock availability, the emergency room plays a vital role in saving lives, managing acute injuries, and delivering prompt medical interventions.

The maternity ward is a place of joy, anticipation, and nurturing care within a hospital. It is where expectant mothers receive specialized medical attention before, during, and after childbirth. The ward is equipped with comfortable birthing suites, monitoring equipment, and skilled obstetricians, midwives, and nurses who support women through the entire birthing process. From prenatal consultations to postnatal care, the maternity ward ensures the well-being of both mother and baby, fostering a safe and welcoming environment for the start of a new life.

The radiology department is an essential component of modern healthcare, utilizing advanced imaging techniques to aid in diagnosis and treatment planning. Equipped with cutting-edge technology such as X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and ultrasound machines, this department plays a crucial role in visualizing internal structures and detecting abnormalities. Radiologists and radiology technologists work collaboratively to capture and interpret images, providing valuable insights that assist physicians in making accurate diagnoses and developing effective treatment strategies for patients across various medical specialties.

Our Branches

Cairo branch

Opend in July 27, 2002

Address: 123 Nile Street, Al-Giza District, Zamalek, Cairo, Egypt

Cairo Hospital is a leading medical facility located in the heart of Egypt's bustling capital city. With its state-of-the-art infrastructure and a team of highly skilled healthcare professionals, the hospital is committed to providing exceptional care to patients. Equipped with cutting-edge technology, Cairo Hospital offers a wide range of specialized services, including advanced diagnostic imaging, surgical procedures, and comprehensive rehabilitation programs. The hospital's compassionate staff and patient-centered approach ensure that individuals receive personalized attention and the highest quality of medical treatment.

Alexandria branch

Opend in March 15, 2005

Address: 456 Corniche Road, Raml Station, Al-Mansheya, Alexandria, Egypt

Situated along the picturesque Corniche Road, Alexandria Hospital stands as a beacon of healthcare excellence in the coastal city. This modern medical center boasts a multidisciplinary team of specialists who are dedicated to delivering top-notch care to patients. From advanced cardiac interventions and minimally invasive surgeries to specialized cancer treatments and comprehensive maternity services, Alexandria Hospital offers a wide array of medical services. With a focus on patient comfort and well-being, the hospital strives to create a healing environment, supported by cutting-edge technology and a compassionate approach to healthcare.

Sharm El Sheikh branch

Opend in November 10, 2010

Address: 101 Naama Bay, South Sinai Governorate, Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt

Nestled amidst the stunning landscapes of Sharm El Sheikh, this hospital serves as a vital healthcare hub for both residents and tourists. With a serene and inviting atmosphere, the hospital caters to the diverse medical needs of the community. Its team of skilled physicians and caring nurses provide a range of services, including emergency care, orthopedics, gynecology, and pediatrics. The hospital's commitment to excellence is evident in its modern facilities, advanced medical equipment, and dedication to delivering personalized, quality care to every patient who walks through its doors.

Hurghada branch

Opend in May 3, 2025

Address: 567 Sheraton Road, Al-Ahiaa, Hurghada, Red Sea Governorate, Egypt

Situated in the vibrant coastal city of Hurghada, the hospital is a trusted healthcare provider for locals and international visitors alike. With a focus on comprehensive medical services, Hurghada Hospital offers specialized departments such as cardiology, neurology, dermatology, and more. The hospital's team of highly trained doctors, nurses, and support staff work collaboratively to ensure the best possible outcomes for patients. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and adhering to international standards, Hurghada Hospital is committed to providing accessible, compassionate, and efficient healthcare services to the community it serves.
